Location

How to Find Us

Nestled in the heart of the Himalayas, our homestay offers the perfect balance of seclusion and accessibility.

Getting Here

By Air

The nearest airport is Pantnagar Airport (PGH), approximately 280 km away. From there, you can hire a taxi to Munsiyari.

By Train

The nearest railway station is Kathgodam, about 260 km away. Regular trains connect Kathgodam to major cities like Delhi. From Kathgodam, you can take a taxi to Munsiyari.

By Road

Munsiyari is well-connected by road. From Munsiyari town, our homestay is a 15-minute drive. We can arrange pickup from Munsiyari on request.

Shuttle Service

We offer a complimentary shuttle service from Munsiyari town to our homestay for all guests. Please inform us of your arrival details in advance.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting and visiting us

What is the best time to contact you?

Our office is open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM (IST) every day. However, you can send us an email or fill out the contact form at any time, and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

How far in advance should I book my stay?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (April-June and September-November). For holiday periods and special occasions, booking 1-2 months ahead is advisable to ensure availability.

Do you arrange transportation from nearby cities?

Yes, we can arrange private transportation from Kathgodam, Pantnagar, or other nearby cities for an additional fee. Please contact us with your travel details, and we'll provide you with options and pricing.

Is there mobile network and internet connectivity at the homestay?

Yes, most major mobile networks have coverage in our area, though signal strength can vary. We provide complimentary WiFi throughout the property, but please note that internet speeds may be slower than in urban areas due to our mountain location.
