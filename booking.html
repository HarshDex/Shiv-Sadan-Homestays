FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about booking your stay

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival. Cancellations made within 7 days of arrival are subject to a one-night charge. No-shows will be charged the full amount of the booking.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

During peak season (April-June and September-November), we have a minimum stay requirement of 2 nights. During other periods, there is no minimum stay requirement, though we recommend at least 2 nights to fully experience the beauty of the region.

Do you offer any discounts for longer stays?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount for stays of 5 nights or more, and a 15% discount for stays of 7 nights or more. These discounts are automatically applied during the booking process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI payments, and bank transfers. For bank transfers, please contact us directly after making your booking.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

Yes, a complimentary breakfast is included in all room rates. We serve a mix of traditional Kumaoni and continental breakfast options, prepared with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Need Help with Your Booking?

Our team is available to assist you with any questions or special requirements you may have.
