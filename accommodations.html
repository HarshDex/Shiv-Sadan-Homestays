Booking Information

Plan Your Stay

We recommend booking your stay at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (April-June and September-November). Our check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM.

Cancellation Policy

Free cancellation up to 7 days before arrival. Cancellations within 7 days of arrival are subject to a one-night charge.

Children of all ages are welcome. One child under 6 years stays free when using existing beds. Extra beds are available for ₹1,500 per night.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our accommodations

What is the best time to visit?

The best time to visit Shiv Sadan is during spring (April-June) when the weather is pleasant and the flowers are in bloom, and autumn (September-November) when the skies are clear and offer spectacular mountain views. Summer months can be rainy, while winter (December-February) brings snow and colder temperatures but offers a magical winter wonderland experience.

How do I reach Shiv Sadan Homestay?

The nearest airport is Pantnagar Airport, approximately 280 km away. From there, you can hire a taxi to Munsiyari. Alternatively, you can take a train to Kathgodam and then a taxi to Munsiyari. From Munsiyari, we provide a complimentary shuttle service to our homestay. We can also arrange private transportation from major cities upon request.

Is the homestay suitable for elderly guests?

While we welcome guests of all ages, please note that our property is located in a mountainous area with some uneven terrain. The Garden Room and Mountain View Suite are more accessible and recommended for elderly guests or those with mobility concerns. Please contact us before booking if you have specific accessibility requirements.

Do you offer meal plans?

Yes, we offer various meal plans. Breakfast is complimentary for all guests. You can opt for half-board (breakfast and dinner) or full-board (all meals) during your stay. Our meals feature authentic Kumaoni cuisine made with locally-sourced ingredients, though we can accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice.

Is there internet connectivity?

Yes, we provide complimentary WiFi throughout the property. However, please note that due to our remote mountain location, internet speeds may be slower than in urban areas, and occasional disruptions can occur during bad weather.
