Our Story

Discover the journey behind Shiv Sadan Homestay

About Us

A Mountain Retreat With A Purpose

Nestled in the heart of the majestic Himalayas, Shiv Sadan Homestay was born from a deep love for the mountains and a desire to share their tranquil beauty with others. Our journey began when we discovered this pristine location with panoramic views of the Panchachuli peaks, just 15 kilometers from Munsiyari in Uttarakhand.

What started as a personal retreat quickly evolved into a vision to create a space where travelers could experience authentic Himalayan hospitality while enjoying modern comforts. We wanted to build a place that honors the natural surroundings while providing a luxurious mountain experience unlike any other.

Our Philosophy

What We Believe In

At Shiv Sadan, we believe that true luxury lies in meaningful experiences, genuine connections, and harmony with nature.

Sustainable Practices

From our eco-friendly construction to our farm-to-table dining, we strive to minimize our environmental footprint and preserve the natural beauty of the Himalayas for generations to come.

Cultural Preservation

We celebrate and share local traditions, crafts, and cuisine with our guests, ensuring that the rich cultural heritage of the Kumaon region continues to thrive and be appreciated.

Community Support

We employ local staff and source materials and produce from nearby villages to support the regional economy and create sustainable livelihoods for the mountain communities.
